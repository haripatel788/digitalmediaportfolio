Video Tribute

Reflection: I enjoyed making the final project becuase it was just a logner version of my favorite after effects project, which was the lyrical video. I chose the song Headlines by Drake since it was one of my favorite songs from Drake and it met the time requirements for the project aswell. I used different After Effect things such as null objects, cameras, track mattes, and other things to help me make the project the best I can. I also chose a black background so that the gold accent of the text can pop out. I chose gold as the main color for the text, becuase on the album cover for this song, gold is very prominent, therefore the gold accents. I also wanted ot make it fun and enjoyable so I added pictures of differnet things heard in the lyrics and used different transitions so that it is visually appealing.
